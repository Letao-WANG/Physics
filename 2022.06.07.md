#### Time dependence of the effective needle

$$

\newline
\dot{\mu_y} = w\mu_x\ \newline
\dot{\mu_z} = 0
$$

$$
\mu_x(t) = \mu_{x0}\text{cos}(wt)-\mu_{y0}\text{sin}(wt)
